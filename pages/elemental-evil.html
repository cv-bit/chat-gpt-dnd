The Temple of the Elemental Evil

A cult is attempting to summon an ancient and powerful evil entity known as the Elemental Evil, and only our heroes can stop them. Gather your strength and join the fight to prevent the summoning and save the world from destruction.

Act 1

The characters receive a warning from a wise old wizard about the cult's plans. They must gather information and allies to prepare for the looming threat.

Act 2

The characters travel to the temple where the cult is performing the summoning ritual, facing challenges and battles along the way. They arrive just in time to interrupt the ritual, but the elemental evil is already partially summoned and begins to wreak havoc.

Act 3

The characters must find a way to banish the elemental evil and stop the cult's plans. Can they overcome the cult's powerful leader and save the world from destruction?

Intro

The Temple of the Elemental Evil: This campaign also takes place in a fantasy world, but with a darker and more ominous tone. The characters must stop a cult from summoning an ancient and powerful evil entity known as the Elemental Evil, which threatens to destroy the world.

loot and items

  • A magical tome containing ancient knowledge and powerful spells that can be used to banish the elemental evil.
  • A holy relic that grants the characters protection from the elemental evil and its minions.
  • A magical amulet that allows the characters to communicate with the elements and call upon their power in battle.
  • A cursed artifact that the cult was using to summon the elemental evil, which the characters can use to reverse the ritual and banish the entity.
  • A powerful and ancient magical weapon that can be used to defeat the cult's leader and their minions.

Npc, Encounters, and locations

  • NPC: The leader of the cult, a powerful and charismatic sorcerer who seeks to summon the elemental evil.
  • NPC: A brave and selfless paladin who joins the characters in their quest to stop the cult and protect the world from the elemental evil.
  • NPC: A group of rebels who are fighting against the cult and seeking to overthrow their leader.
  • NPC: A traitor within the group who is secretly working for the cult and trying to sabotage the characters' efforts.
  • NPC: A group of innocent villagers who are being terrorized by the cult and seek the characters' help to defeat them.
  • NPC: A wise and powerful druid who helps the characters understand the nature of the elemental evil and how to defeat it.
  • Encounter: A group of cultists who try to sacrifice the characters to the elemental evil during a ritual.
  • Encounter: An elemental monster summoned by the cult that attacks the characters and tries to prevent them from interrupting the summoning ritual.
  • Encounter: A group of elementals summoned by the cult to defend the temple and prevent the characters from stopping the summoning ritual.
  • Encounter: A giant golem animated by the cult to serve as a guardian for the temple.
  • Encounter: A group of elementals summoned by the cult to attack the characters and protect the temple.
  • Encounter: A powerful and ancient dragon that has been awakened by the cult's summoning ritual and now seeks to destroy them all.
  • Location: A secret underground chamber where the cult is performing the summoning ritual.
  • Location: A mountain pass leading to the temple, guarded by fierce winter storms and hostile yeti.
  • Location: A haunted and abandoned graveyard, filled with the ghosts of those who have fallen victim to the cult's rituals.
  • Location: A dense and overgrown forest, home to powerful fey beings who may or may not help the characters in their quest.
